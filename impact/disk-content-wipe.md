Disk Content Wipe
=================
Malware may erase the content of storage devices.

This description refines the ATT&CK [**Disk Wipe::Disk Content Wipe](https://attack.mitre.org/techniques/T1561/001/) sub-technique.

|Name|ID|Description|
|---|---|---|
